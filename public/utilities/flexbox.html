Flexbox

Utility classes for flexbox.

Flex container

Utilities for flex containers.

Class Description
.display-flex

A block-level flexbox container.
.display-inlineFlex

An inline flexbox container.
.fd-row

Default. The flexible items are displayed horizontally, as a row.
.fd-rowReverse

Same as row, but in reverse order.
.fd-column

The flexible items are displayed vertically, as a column.
.fd-columnReverse

Same as column, but in reverse order
.fw-nowrap

Default. Specifies that the flexible items will not wrap.
.fw-wrap

Specifies that the flexible items will wrap if necessary.
.fw-wrapReverse

Specifies that the flexible items will wrap, if necessary, in reverse order.
.ai-stretch

Default. Items are stretched to fit the container.
.ai-flexStart

Items are positioned at the beginning of the container.
.ai-flexEnd

Items are positioned at the end of the container.
.ai-center

Items are positioned at the center of the container.
.ai-baseline

Items are positioned at the baseline of the container.
.jc-flexStart

Default. Items are positioned at the beginning of the container.
.jc-flexEnd

Items are positioned at the end of the container.
.jc-center

Items are positioned at the center of the container.
.jc-spaceBetween

Items are positioned with space between the lines.
.jc-spaceAround

Distribute items evenly. Items have equal space around them.
.jc-spaceEvenly

Apply the 5th value in the leading scale
.ac-flexStart

Lines are packed toward the start of the flex container.
.ac-flexEnd

Lines are packed toward the end of the flex container.
.ac-center

Lines are packed toward the center of the flex container.
.ac-spaceBetween

Lines are evenly distributed in the flex container.
.ac-spaceAround

Lines are evenly distributed in the flex container, with half-size spaces on either end.
.ac-stretch

Default. Lines stretch to take up the remaining space.

Flex item

Utilities for flex items.

Class Description
.as-flexStart

The flex item is positioned at the beginning of the container.
.as-flexEnd

The flex item is positioned at the end of the container.
.as-center

The flex item is positioned at the center of the container.
.as-baseline

The flex item is positioned at the baseline of the container.
.as-stretch

The flex item is positioned to fit the container.
.fg-0

Prevent a flex item from growing.
.fg-1

Allow a flex item to grow to fill any available space.
.fs-0

Prevent a flex item from shrinking.
.fs-1

Allow a flex item to shrink if necessary.
.fb-0

Set flex-basis to 0.
.fb-auto

Default. The length of the flex item is according to its content.
.flex-auto

Allow a flex item to grow and shrink, according to its initial size. Same as 1 1 auto.
.flex-initial

Allow a flex item to shrink but not grow, according to its initial size. Same as 0 1 auto.
.flex-none

Prevent a flex item from both growing and shrinking. Same as 0 0 auto.
.flex-1

Allow a flex item to grow and shrink, ignoring its initial size.
