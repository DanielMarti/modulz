Section

Sections represent distinct areas of a screen—e.g. marketing site sections.

Default section

A Section component can contain almost content but usually contains a main Heading. Sections have no background color by default.

Sizes

There are three size variations. Smaller sizes work well with more compact content with a tighter focus. Larger sizes work well with sparse content and looser content layouts.
