Glossary

A collection of the various terminology used throughout Modulz design system.

Term Meaning
Hue Hue is one of the main properties of a color, defined technically as the degree to which a stimulus can be described as different from other stimuli.
Saturation The colorfulness of an area judged in proportion to its brightness, which in effect is the perceived freedom from whitishness of the light coming from the area.
Lightness Lightness is a representation of variation in the perception of a color's brightness.
